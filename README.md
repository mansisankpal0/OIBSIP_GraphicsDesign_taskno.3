Objective:
The goal of this project was to design a professional business card for BoldBrush, a creative design brand. Business cards serve as both an introduction and an expression of branding, so the design focuses on capturing the company’s essence, typography, and ingenuity.

Design Process:
1.Understanding Branding
  -Incorporated BoldBrush’s brand identity and slogan: “Bold Ideas, Beautiful Designs.”
  -Ensured alignment with the company’s creative and modern values.
2.Typography & Layout
  -Selected clean and elegant fonts for readability.
  -Balanced the design with structured spacing and symmetry.
3.Color & Visuals
  -Used a blue & gold palette to reflect professionalism and creativity.
  -Added subtle abstract background patterns to create depth.
4.Final Touches
  -Ensured scalability and clarity for both print and digital sharing.
  -Exported in PNG (digital) and PDF (print-ready) formats.

Outcome:
A modern and professional business card that:
  -Strengthens brand identity 
  -Provides clear and accessible contact details 
  -Leaves a lasting impression
